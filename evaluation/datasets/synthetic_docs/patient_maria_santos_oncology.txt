Clinical Summary:
Mrs. Santos, a 52-year-old female, presented with progressive cervical lymphadenopathy, night sweats, and unintentional weight loss of 8 kg over two months. Excisional biopsy of a right cervical lymph node performed on February 25 at St. Marcus Medical Center confirmed Diffuse Large B-Cell Lymphoma (DLBCL), germinal center B-cell subtype, Ki-67 proliferation index 85%.

Staging Workup:
PET-CT scan (March 2) revealed hypermetabolic lymphadenopathy in cervical, mediastinal, and para-aortic regions (SUVmax 18.4). Bone marrow biopsy was negative for lymphoma involvement. Final staging: Stage IIIA.

Treatment Plan:
Dr. Sharma recommended 6 cycles of R-CHOP (Rituximab, Cyclophosphamide, Doxorubicin, Vincristine, Prednisone) administered every 21 days. Cycle 1 was administered on March 10 without complications. Pre-chemotherapy labs showed adequate organ function.

Supportive Care: Ondansetron for antiemetic prophylaxis, Pegfilgrastim for neutropenia prevention. Social work referral placed for financial counseling given insurance complications.
